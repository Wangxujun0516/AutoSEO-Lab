## 2. CE Certification Requirements

### 2.1 CE Marking Overview

The CE marking indicates compliance with EU health, safety, and environmental protection standards. For industrial equipment, the relevant directives are:

| Directive | Scope |
|-----------|-------|
| **2006/42/EC** | Machinery Directive |
| **2014/30/EU** | Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC) |
| **2014/35/EU** | Low Voltage Directive (LVD) |
| **2015/863/EU** | RoHS (Restriction of Hazardous Substances) |

### 2.2 Technical Documentation Requirements

According to the Machinery Directive (2006/42/EC), the technical file must include:

| Document | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Risk Assessment** | Systematic analysis of hazards and risks |
| **Technical Specifications** | Drawings, diagrams, component lists |
| **Test Reports** | EMC, LVD, and performance test results |
| **User Manual** | Installation, operation, maintenance instructions |
| **Declaration of Conformity** | Signed by the manufacturer or authorized representative |

### 2.3 EU Authorized Representative

For non-EU manufacturers:
- Must appoint an EU-based Authorized Representative
- The representative must be identified on the product labeling
- The representative's contact details must be included in the technical documentation

## 3. OSHA Compliance for Industrial Equipment

### 3.1 OSHA General Industry Standards

| Standard | Title | Key Requirements |
|----------|-------|------------------|
| **29 CFR 1910.132** | Personal Protective Equipment | PPE requirements for workers |
| **29 CFR 1910.133** | Eye and Face Protection | Safety glasses, face shields |
| **29 CFR 1910.147** | Lockout/Tagout | Energy control procedures |
| **29 CFR 1910.212** | Machine Guarding | Guarding requirements for machinery |
| **29 CFR 1910.217** | Mechanical Power Presses | Safety standards for presses |
| **29 CFR 1910.303** | Electrical Installations | Wiring methods and materials |

### 3.2 OSHA Machine Guarding Requirements

**General Requirements**:
- Point-of-operation guards must prevent operator access to hazardous areas
- Guards must be attached to the machine or secured in place
- Guards must not create additional hazards
- Interlocking devices must prevent machine operation when guards are removed

**Power Transmission Guarding**:
- All belts, pulleys, gears, sprockets, and chains must be guarded
- Guards must be of substantial construction
- Guard openings must be small enough to prevent hand/finger insertion

## 6. Procurement Decision Framework

### 6.1 Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) Model

```
TCO = Purchase Price + Logistics + Installation + Maintenance + Downtime + Disposal
```

**Key TCO Components**:

| Component | Description | Weight |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| **Purchase Price** | Unit cost from supplier | 30% |
| **Logistics** | Shipping, customs, duties | 15% |
| **Installation** | Labor and integration costs | 10% |
| **Maintenance** | Routine service and repairs | 20% |
| **Downtime** | Production loss due to failures | 20% |
| **Disposal** | End-of-life handling | 5% |

## 7. Request for Quotation (RFQ) Template

### 7.1 RFQ Structure

1. **Company Information**
   - Company name and contact details
   - Authorized signatory

2. **Product Specifications**
   - Product description and technical requirements
   - Quantity and delivery schedule
   - Acceptance criteria

3. **Quality Requirements**
   - Certifications required
   - Testing and inspection procedures
   - Quality control standards

4. **Commercial Terms**
   - Price and payment terms
   - Delivery terms (Incoterms)
   - Warranty and liability

5. **Documentation Requirements**
   - Technical documentation
   - Certificates and test reports
   - Packing and labeling requirements

6. **Evaluation Criteria**
   - Scoring methodology
   - Weighting factors

7. **Timeline**
   - RFQ issuance date
   - Deadline for responses
   - Expected award date

### 7.2 Sample RFQ Checklist

- [ ] Product specifications with dimensional drawings
- [ ] Quantity breakdown and delivery schedule
- [ ] Required certifications (CE, ISO, etc.)
- [ ] Testing requirements and acceptance criteria
- [ ] Price quotation in USD/EUR
- [ ] Payment terms (T/T, L/C, etc.)
- [ ] Incoterms (FOB, CIF, EXW)
- [ ] Lead time for prototype and production
- [ ] MOQ and volume pricing
- [ ] Warranty terms and conditions
- [ ] Return and replacement policy
- [ ] Technical support and after-sales service
- [ ] Samples availability and cost
- [ ] Packaging requirements
- [ ] Labeling requirements
- [ ] Shipping documents required
- [ ] Certificate of Origin requirements
- [ ] Insurance requirements
